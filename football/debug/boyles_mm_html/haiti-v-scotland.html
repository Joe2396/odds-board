        One of the major storylines heading into the match is Haiti’s return to the World Cup Stage as the Caribbean nation has not appeared at the tournament since 1974. As it is also Scotland's first game in the competition since 1998, too, this football betting fixture puts decades of waiting behind for both of these countries.


        Haiti Vs Scotland World Cup 2026 Preview 


        Scotland enter the fixture as favourites, with Steve Clarke’s side arriving in the United States with plenty of confidence after topping their qualification group. With experienced players throughout the squad and goal scorers coming from midfield, Scotland are expected to start the tournament with a victory.


        However, Haiti should not be overlooked as they also finished top of the qualifying group to secure their place at the World Cup Finals. They will view this as a chance to make an immediate impression on their return, and while they are considered among the tournaments' smaller nations, they have called up numerous players with Haitian heritage who could bring some extra quality to the pitch.


        This will be the first ever meeting between Haiti and Scotland, with there being no previous encounters between the nations. Therefore, both teams will be entering relatively unknown territory when they face off, but will still want to start their group stage campaigns strongly following their long absences from the World Cup.
