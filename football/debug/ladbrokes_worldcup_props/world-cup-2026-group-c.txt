Group C Odds And Betting Markets For The World Cup 2026
Group C odds

Group C at the 2026 World Cup features one of the tournament favourites in Brazil, who arrive with a squad packed full of attacking talent and expectations of progressing comfortably to the knockout stages.

Morocco head into the tournament looking to build on their impressive recent performances on the international stage, while Scotland will believe they have a strong chance of competing for qualification. Haiti complete the group as determined underdogs capable of making things difficult for their opponents.

With Brazil expected to lead the way and a competitive battle developing behind them for qualification, Group C promises several fascinating contests throughout the group stage.

Group C betting

The primary betting focus in Group C centres around the group winner and teams to qualify markets.

Brazil’s attacking quality and squad depth make them overwhelming favourites to top the group, while Morocco and Scotland are expected to battle for the remaining qualification place. Morocco’s defensive structure and recent tournament pedigree could make them an appealing option in qualification betting, while Scotland may attract support in match betting and outsider markets.

Fixtures involving Brazil are also likely to feature heavily in goalscorer and total goals betting markets throughout the group stage.

Who are the favourites to win Group C?

Brazil are widely considered the favourites to win Group C, with their attacking depth and tournament pedigree making them one of the standout teams in the competition. Morocco and Scotland are expected to compete closely for qualification, while Haiti will be aiming to cause an upset and challenge for points.

When are Group C's matches at the 2026 World Cup?

Group C matches take place between 13 June and 24 June 2026, with all kick-off times in BST:

Brazil vs Morocco – 13 June (23:00 BST)
Haiti vs Scotland – 14 June (02:00 BST)
Scotland vs Morocco – 19 June (23:00 BST)
Brazil vs Haiti – 20 June (01:00 BST)
Scotland vs Brazil – 24 June (23:00 BST)
Morocco vs Haiti – 24 June (23:00 BST)
