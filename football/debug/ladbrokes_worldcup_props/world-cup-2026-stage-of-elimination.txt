Stage Of Elimination Odds - 2026 World Cup Betting
2026 World Cup stage of elimination odds

Stage of elimination betting offers a different way to follow the 2026 World Cup, allowing customers to predict exactly how far each nation will progress during the tournament.

Rather than simply backing the outright winner, these markets cover every possible outcome - from teams being eliminated in the group stage to reaching the quarter-finals, semi-finals, final, or going all the way and lifting the trophy.

With so many top nations expected to challenge deep into the competition, stage of elimination betting can provide added interest throughout every phase of the World Cup.

How stage of elimination betting works

These markets are based on the exact round a team exits the tournament.

For example, customers can back a nation to:

Be knocked out in the group stage
Reach the round of 32
Progress as far as the round of 16
Exit in the quarter-finals
Suffer defeat in the semi-finals
Lose in the final
Win the World Cup

Markets like these are especially popular for teams expected to go deep into the tournament, including France, England, Spain, Argentina, and Brazil.

You can also bet on each of the latter stages individually this summer, with Ladbrokes offering betting markets on which nations will reach the round of 16, the quarter-finals, the semi-finals, and the final.

Which teams are expected to progress furthest?

France currently lead the outright World Cup betting, alongside Spain, while England, Argentina, and Brazil are also among the leading contenders to reach the latter stages.

The expanded 48-team format could also create opportunities for surprise packages to progress further than expected, making stage of elimination betting one of the most interesting outright markets available throughout the tournament.
