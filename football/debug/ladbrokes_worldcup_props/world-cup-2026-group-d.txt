Group D Odds And Betting Markets For The World Cup 2026
Group D odds

Group D has the look of a genuinely unpredictable section at the 2026 World Cup, with no clear separation between the four teams chasing progression.

USA come into the tournament with expectation on their shoulders as co-hosts, and their familiarity with the conditions could give them an important advantage. However, they are unlikely to have things all their own way in a group packed with capable opposition.

Turkey bring a mix of experience and emerging talent, while Paraguay arrive with renewed confidence after returning to the World Cup for the first time in over a decade. Australia, meanwhile, have quietly built a reputation for staying competitive against strong opposition in recent months and won’t be short of belief.

This is a group where momentum could shift quickly, and a single result may prove decisive.

Group D betting markets

Group D presents a competitive betting angle, particularly in the race for qualification where the gap between the sides appears minimal.

USA’s home advantage makes them a natural favourite to top the group, but Turkey’s technical quality and attacking options ensure they remain firmly in contention. Paraguay’s recent performances suggest they could be a difficult opponent for anyone in the group, while Australia’s resilience gives them a chance of picking up important results.

With no standout mismatch, results in this group could hinge on fine details, making each fixture crucial in shaping the final standings.

Who enters Group D as favourites?

USA are widely considered the favourites to top Group D, with home advantage and squad depth giving them an edge. Turkey are expected to provide the main challenge, while Paraguay and Australia are both seen as capable of pushing for qualification.

Which players could make the difference in Group D?

Key players are likely to have a major influence in such a competitive group. USA will look to Christian Pulisic for attacking inspiration, while Turkey boast creative talent in Hakan Çalhanoğlu and Arda Güler. Paraguay’s attacking threat includes Miguel Almirón and Julio Enciso, while Australia rely on the experience of Mat Ryan alongside the exciting talent of Nestory Irankunda.

When are Group D matches being played at the 2026 World Cup?

Group D matches take place between 13 June and 26 June 2026, with all kick-off times in BST:

USA vs Paraguay – 13 June (02:00 BST)
Australia vs Turkey – 14 June (05:00 BST)
USA vs Australia – 19 June (20:00 BST)
Turkey vs Paraguay – 20 June (04:00 BST)
Paraguay vs Australia – 26 June (03:00 BST)
Turkey vs USA – 26 June (03:00 BST)

The final round of fixtures will both be played simultaneously, with qualification for the knockout stages potentially on the line for all four teams.
