Top Goalscorer Odds And Betting Markets At The 2026 World Cup
2026 World Cup top goalscorer odds

The race for the Golden Boot at the 2026 World Cup is shaping up to be one of the most competitive in recent memory, with a host of elite forwards expected to lead the scoring charts.

France’s Kylian Mbappé heads the early market as the standout contender, followed closely by England captain Harry Kane, who has consistently delivered on the international stage. Argentina’s Lionel Messi remains firmly in the conversation, while Norway’s Erling Haaland brings a relentless goalscoring presence into the tournament.

Spain are well represented with both Lamine Yamal and Mikel Oyarzabal featuring prominently, while experience also comes into play with Cristiano Ronaldo still capable of making an impact. Belgium’s Romelu Lukaku and Brazil’s Vinícius Jr complete a stacked list of leading names expected to challenge for the Golden Boot this summer.

Top goalscorer betting

Betting on the Golden Boot isn’t just about picking the most prolific striker - it often comes down to factors such as team progression, penalty duties, and overall attacking output.

Players from nations expected to go deep into the tournament, such as France, England, Brazil, and Argentina, typically dominate this market. That gives the likes of Mbappé, Kane, Messi, and Vinícius Jr a natural advantage, while players like Haaland and Alexander Isak could offer value if their teams outperform expectations.

Who are the favourites for the Golden Boot?

Kylian Mbappé currently leads the way in the top goalscorer market, with Harry Kane and Lionel Messi close behind. Erling Haaland is also expected to challenge, while Spain’s attacking options - plus a host of other experienced names - ensure there’s no shortage of competition.

With so many high-quality forwards involved, the Golden Boot race is likely to go down to the latter stages of the tournament.

Could goalscoring records be broken at the 2026 World Cup?

There is added intrigue around the all-time World Cup goalscoring record this summer. Miroslav Klose’s record is within reach, with both Lionel Messi and Kylian Mbappé having the opportunity to close in on - or potentially surpass - that historic milestone of 16 goals.

If either player goes deep into the tournament and finds form in front of goal, history could be made.
