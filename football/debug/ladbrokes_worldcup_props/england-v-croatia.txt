England Vs Croatia - 2026 World Cup Betting & Odds
England vs Croatia odds & preview

England begin their 2026 World Cup campaign with a heavyweight clash in Group L against Croatia on Wednesday, 17 June, with kick-off scheduled for 21:00 BST.

It’s a fixture loaded with recent World Cup history - with that dramatic semi-final in 2018 still fresh in the memory - and one that could immediately shape the direction of the group. England arrive with high expectations after strong performances at recent major tournaments, while Croatia once again bring proven tournament pedigree and a squad packed with international experience.

With both nations expected to challenge for qualification from Group L, victory here could provide crucial momentum heading into the rest of the group stage - and it's England who are tipped as favourites to get their World Cup off to the perfect start.

England vs Croatia betting markets

This World Cup opener offers plenty of betting opportunities, with markets available across everything from match outcomes and goals to player cards and in-play options.

England’s attacking depth and Croatia’s technical quality should create opportunities at both ends of the pitch, while the intensity of a major tournament opener could also bring disciplinary markets into focus.

Could England start with a win against Croatia?

Opening matches can often define the mood of an entire World Cup campaign, and England will be aiming to begin Group L with a statement result.

Croatia have proven they can compete on the biggest stage over the last decade, but England’s blend of tournament experience, attacking quality, and squad depth makes them one of the standout contenders at this summer’s competition.

A strong performance here could immediately strengthen England’s outright World Cup credentials heading into the remaining group fixtures. Check out our in-depth England preview here in the build up to their opening World Cup match.

England vs Croatia takes place on Wednesday, 17 June 2026, with kick-off at 21:00 BST.
