Champions League Betting

The Champions League is Europe’s premier club competition, bringing together the continent’s biggest teams in an expanded league-format tournament. After the qualifying rounds, 36 clubs enter the league phase, where each team plays eight opponents. The top-ranked sides progress directly to the Round of 16, while teams placed in the mid-table positions compete in play-offs to join them. From there, the tournament continues with traditional two-legged knockout rounds, delivering months of high-quality, high-stakes, high-drama football.

At William Hill, we have pre-tournament odds under the Champions League betting, plus a host of other odds throughout the tournament, from group winners to top goalscorers.

Our traders also have options for the Europa League, Europa Conference League, European Super Cup and the FIFA Club World Cup: whatever cup competition you're looking for, William Hill have a market for it.

Cristiano Ronaldo is the competition's all-time top-scorer with 140 goals in 183 appearances for Manchester United, Real Madrid and Juventus.

FA Cup Final

The showpiece event of the English football season, the FA Cup final is one of the oldest and most-watched cup competitions on the planet. The FA Cup started in 1871 and features over 600 teams trying to make it down to the final 124 at the first-round stage, on the road to a Wembley final.

The FA Cup is the only competition in the country where non-league minnows can end up going against some of the biggest and best clubs in the world. We offer a huge range of FA Cup betting markets from the first round right through to the semi-finals and final at Wembley Stadium.

Qualification for the FA Cup begins as early as August with the extra preliminary round. The first round proper takes place in November, with Premier League and Championship teams entering the competition at the third-round stage in January. The final is slated for May.
