When did England last play Ghana?

England beat Ghana 2-1 at the 2014 World Cup in Brazil. Daniel Sturridge and Wayne Rooney scored for England, with Andre Ayew replying for Ghana.

What is Ghana's best World Cup performance?

Ghana reached the quarter-finals in 2010, narrowly losing to Uruguay on penalties. Luis Suarez's infamous handball denied Ghana a winning goal in extra time.

How many World Cups have Ghana qualified for?

Ghana have qualified for 4 World Cups: 2006, 2010, 2014, and 2022. They are one of Africa's most consistent qualifiers over the past two decades.

How much could I win by betting on Panama vs England

If you bet £10 on Panama to beat England at the current odds of 10.0, you would win £100. On the other hand, if you bet £10 on England to beat Panama at the current odds of 1.25, you would win £12.5.

Who is Ghana's all-time top scorer?

Asamoah Gyan holds the record with 51 goals in 109 appearances. He remains Africa's all-time top World Cup scorer with 6 goals.
