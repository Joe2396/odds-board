Netherlands v Japan Betting & Odds

If you have a hunch about the outcome of the fixture between Netherlands and Japan, have a punt on it! We have odds on 89 different popular markets for Netherlands v Japan.

Where can I bet on Netherlands v Japan?

Bet on the Football fixture between Netherlands and Japan, which starts on Sunday, 14 June, at 20:00.

How much could I win by betting on Netherlands v Japan?

If you bet £10 on Netherlands to beat Japan at the current odds of 1.95, you would win £19.5. On the other hand, if you bet £10 on Japan to beat Netherlands at the current odds of 3.7, you would win £37.

What are the other Netherlands v Japan betting markets?

You can bet on other FIFA World Cup popular markets such as: Correct Score, Over/Under 0.5 Goals, Over/Under 1.5 Goals, Over/Under 2.5 Goals, Over/Under 3.5 Goals.
