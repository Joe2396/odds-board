Bet on the Football fixture between Argentina and Austria, which starts on Monday, 22 June, at 17:00.

How much could I win by betting on Argentina v Austria?

If you bet £10 on Argentina to beat Austria at the current odds of 1.57, you would win £15.7. On the other hand, if you bet £10 on Austria to beat Argentina at the current odds of 5.5, you would win £55.

How much could I win by betting on Argentina v Austria?
