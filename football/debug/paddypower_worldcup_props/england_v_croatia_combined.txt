When did England last beat Croatia in a major tournament?

England beat Croatia 1-0 at Euro 2020. Raheem Sterling's second-half goal secured three points in the group stage opener at Wembley.

Why is England vs Croatia considered a rivalry?

The 2018 World Cup semi-final loss still stings for England fans. Croatia's extra-time victory denied England a first final since 1966, creating lasting tension between the sides.

How does Croatia's midfield compare to England's?

Croatia's midfield is their strength, featuring Modrić, Kovačić, and Brozović. England counters with younger energy through Bellingham, Rice, and Foden.

How much could I win by betting on England vs Croatia

If you bet £10 on England to beat Croatia at the current odds of 1.7, you would win £17. On the other hand, if you bet £10 on Croatia to beat England at the current odds of 5.0, you would win £50.

Are England vs Croatia matches high-scoring?

Not typically. Five of their last seven meetings produced under 2.5 goals. Both teams tend to play cautiously in tournament settings.
