WHAT ARE BETTING ODDS?

Odds indicate the likelihood of something happening and in turn, allow you to calculate potential returns.

Higher odds mean higher potential winnings but lower chances of success. Lower odds mean lower potential winnings but higher chances of success.

Low Betting Odds (Favourites):

The league champions winning a match with odds of 1.25 (1/4).
A multi-time grand slam winner coming first with odds of 1.40 (2/5).
A popular horse with odds of 1.15 (3/20).

High Betting Odds (Underdogs):

A lower-ranked rugby team winning with odds of 5.00 (4/1).
An underdog boxer winning a fight with odds of 12.00 (11/1).
A less-favoured tennis player winning with odds of 8.00 (7/1).
